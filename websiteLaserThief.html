Laser Thief:

Laser Thief is the ultimate heist game for your VR headset. Sneak your way through high-tech security systems, dodging lasers to pull off the perfect robbery. The stakes get higher as you progress, with faster lasers and more elaborate traps. Keep low, crawl, and dash past the beams to make your getaway!


Power Ups:

With a hilarious narrator guiding you, you'll be cracking up as you crack safes and evade laser beams. The interactive tutorial will have you mastering the art of thievery in no time!

This game is available in the Oculus Store.
