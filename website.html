Subway Sprinters:

Subway Sprinters is the ultimate endless running game for your vr headset. Run as far as possible to escape from the police. The longer you run, the faster you become. On the way, avoid the trains and buses by changing lanes. Duck and move your head to avoid wooden crates on your current lane. You can also jump by pulling your arms up quickly. With the help of wooden ladders, you can run on the roofs of the trains. Collect as many coins and diamonds as possible to upgrade your power ups. Use shields to protect yourself from colliding with an obstacle.


Power Ups:

• Diamond: Gives you a great boost at the beginning of the game.
• Jetpack: Fly with the jetpack and collect coins.
• Magnet: Collect even more coins by pulling them with the magnet.
• Big jump: Make a big jump and fly through the coin sky!
• Shield: Protects you from obstacles.

With the interactive tutorial, you will quickly understand the game and be able to start playing right away!

This game is available in the Oculus Store.
